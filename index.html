Frequently asked questions

Is it free?

Yes — Continue AI is completely free, with no account and no paywall. Every shipped feature is unlocked.

Do you read or store my conversations?

No. Capturing and packaging a conversation happens entirely on your device. There are no servers of ours, nothing is transmitted to us, and there's no account or telemetry. A conversation only goes to another AI when you choose to transfer it — and then it goes to that AI, not to us.

Which AI tools does it work with?

You capture on ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini, and continue on any of eight — those three plus DeepSeek, Perplexity, Copilot, Grok, and Google AI Studio.

Where is my data, and what if I uninstall?

Everything stays in your browser's local storage, on your own machine. You can export any conversation as PDF, HTML, an image, Markdown, or JSON at any time. Uninstalling removes the extension's local data.

Does it work on really long chats?

Yes. Capture grabs the full thread — it's built to handle long, virtualized conversations where the page only keeps part of the chat in view, so messages aren't silently dropped.
