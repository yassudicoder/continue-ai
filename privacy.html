Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

In short: Continue AI runs on your own device. It has no account, no servers of ours, and no telemetry. Capturing and packaging a conversation happens locally — nothing is transmitted to us. Your conversations never leave your device unless you choose to transfer one, and then it goes to the AI you picked (ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini), not to us.

What the extension stores

Continue AI saves what you capture — your conversations and your settings (such as your destination AI and size preferences) — using your browser's local extension storage (chrome.storage.local). This data lives on your device.

What the extension does not do

  • It does not send your conversations to us. There are no servers of ours, and capturing, compressing, and packaging happen entirely on your device.
  • It does not require an account or sign-in.
  • It has no telemetry, analytics, or tracking, and no advertising trackers.

Transferring a conversation

The one time content leaves your device is when you transfer a conversation. Continue AI packages the chat into a prompt and hands it to the destination AI you chose — ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini — so you can continue there. That happens at your request, the conversation goes to that AI (under its own privacy policy), and it never passes through any server of ours.

Exporting a copy

You can export any conversation as a PDF, HTML page, image, Markdown, or JSON file. Exports are generated on your device and saved wherever you choose; they aren't sent anywhere.

Permissions and why they're needed

  • Storage — to save your captured conversations and settings locally on your device.
  • Side panel — to show Continue AI's interface next to the chat you're viewing.
  • Scripting + access to chatgpt.com, claude.ai, and gemini.google.com — so the extension can read the conversation you're on when you capture it, and place the packaged prompt for you when you continue elsewhere. It reads a conversation only when you ask it to.

Your data and your control

Because everything is stored locally, uninstalling the extension removes its data from your device. You can also export any conversation at any time (PDF, HTML, image, Markdown, or JSON) to keep your own copy. Clearing your browser's extension data will remove it as well.

Third parties

Continue AI does not share your data with third parties, because it does not collect or transmit your conversations in the first place. The only data flow is the transfer you initiate yourself, directly to the AI you chose.

About this website

This marketing website is separate from the extension. It uses no analytics and no tracking, and sets no advertising cookies. It does not track you across other sites.

Children's privacy

Continue AI is a general productivity tool and is not directed to children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the date at the top of this page. Material changes will be reflected in the extension's store listing.
